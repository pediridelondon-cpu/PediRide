PROPOSED SOLUTION

What We Propose to Build

Operator-funded digital infrastructure providing TfL with compliance oversight, automated reporting, and enforcement tools at zero procurement cost

Real-Time Compliance Dashboard

TfL receives read-only dashboard access showing compliance status across the licensed fleet

Data Fields Provided:
  • • DBS check validity dates & expiry alerts
  • • Insurance policy numbers & coverage status
  • • Vehicle registration & inspection records
  • • Driver licence verification & suspension flags
  • • Complaint history & violation tracking
Access Method:
Secure web portal with role-based permissions for TfL officers
Update Frequency:
Real-time updates when operators log compliance actions

Automated Regulatory Reporting

One-click export of compliance and industry data in formats specified by Act 2024

Report Types Available:
  • • Quarterly ride volume & fare reports (anonymized)
  • • Operator compliance audit trails
  • • Geographic heatmaps for route planning
  • • Passenger complaint aggregation logs
  • • Insurance & DBS expiry forecasts
Export Formats:
CSV, Excel, PDF reports - customizable to TfL data standards
Data Privacy:
Anonymized passenger data, operator-specific compliance viewable only by TfL

Westminster Geofencing

GPS-based quiet zone enforcement reducing noise complaints through digital prevention

Technical Implementation:
  • • GPS tracking via driver mobile app
  • • Automatic alerts when entering restricted zones
  • • Time-based restrictions (e.g., post-11pm quiet zones)
  • • Movement tracking to prevent loitering violations
Zone Configuration:
Westminster Council defines geofenced boundaries - we enforce digitally
Violation Logging:
Timestamped GPS evidence for Council enforcement follow-up

DATA GOVERNANCE & SECURITY

GDPR-Compliant, Secure, Auditable

What TfL Receives

  • Anonymized Industry Statistics
    Aggregated ride volumes, fare averages, route heatmaps—no personally identifiable passenger data
  • Operator-Specific Compliance Status
    Individual fleet operator compliance scores (DBS, insurance, vehicle standards) for enforcement targeting
  • Complaint & Violation Logs
    Timestamped records linked to operator IDs for enforcement follow-up, including GPS evidence where available

Data Protection Standards

  • ISO 27001 Compliant Hosting
    AWS UK-based infrastructure with AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit
  • ICO Registration & GDPR Compliance
    Full Data Protection Impact Assessments completed, ICO registered as data processor
  • Audit Trail Transparency
    Full logging of TfL officer dashboard access for accountability and FOIA compliance

Data Sharing Agreement

We propose establishing a formal Data Processing Agreement (DPA) between PediRide and TfL defining:

  • Specific data fields TfL receives vs retains commercially
  • Access permissions and role-based authentication protocols
  • Data retention periods aligned with Act 2024 requirements
  • Breach notification procedures and incident response
