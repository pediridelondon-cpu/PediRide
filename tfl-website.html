IMPLEMENTATION

How PediRide Enables Act 2024 Enforcement

Three-step integration providing immediate compliance visibility

1

Operator Onboarding

Fleet operators subscribe to PediRide OS (£35/vehicle/month) to meet Act 2024 compliance requirements. They upload DBS certificates, insurance policies, and vehicle registrations to our platform.

2

Real-Time Data Flow

As operators process rides via PediRide Pay (QR payments), we capture ride logs, fare data, GPS routes, and passenger ratings—automatically formatted for TfL reporting requirements.

3

TfL Dashboard Access

TfL receives read-only dashboard access showing compliance status, violation alerts, and one-click export of anonymized industry data for transport planning and enforcement priorities.
